Tracey and Jamie

{{site.about-author}}

Residing with our two Eurasiers in Calgary AB. Our experience goes back to the early 2000’s, with the first introduction of Eurasiers to Canada with Wayne Nelson (Sunwolf Eurasiers).

Our breeding journey started in 2019 with Maple (Bellus Belinde) and hope to continue with Sunny (Sunwolf’s Norr Solsken).

We are members of Canadian Eurasier Club, Canadian Kennel Club and have completed the KZG Eurasier Breeding Course.

We believe in the preservation of this amazing breed.

We follow the Early Neurological Stimulation and continue with as many sensory stimulation situations as possible to lay the foundation of well balanced, trusting and confident puppies.
